Peanut Butter Chocolate Chunk Bars

Recipe Description

The following Steps is how you make a Peanut Butter Choclate Chunk Bar:

\

Ingridients

The following Ingridients are needed:

  • Three quarter cups of butter, softened
  • Half cup white sugar
  • Half cup firmly packed briown sugar
  • One egg
  • One teaspoon vanilla extract, or more to tatste
  • One cup peanut butter
  • One Three quarter cups unbleached flour
  • Three quarters teaspoon baking soda
  • 10 ounces chocolate chunks (such as Ghirardelli Bittersweet Choclate

Recipe Direction

  1. Preheat oen to 375 degress F (190 degrees
  2. Beat butter, while sugar, and brown sugar together with an electric mixer in a large bowl until light and fluffy. beat egg and vanilla extract into the butter mixtue. Add peanut butter and beat until incorporated.
  3. Sift flour and baking soda together in a seperate bowl. Mix into the butter mixture. Fold choclate chunks into the mixture.
  4. Spread the batter into a 13 X 9-inch baking dish.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, 20 to 25 minutes. Cool completely before cutting into squares.
