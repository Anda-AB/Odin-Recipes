Easy-Coleslaw-Dressing

Description

I'll be giving a step-by-step direction on making an Easy-Coleslaw-Dressing.

Per serving Has 94 Calories; protein 0.2g; carbohydrates 9.3g; fat 6.5g; cholesterol 5.1mg; sodium 236.2mg.

Ingredients

The following Ingredients are needed:

  • Half cup mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • Half tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 Table spoon vinegar
  • Half tablespoon ground black pepper
  • quarter teaspoon salt

Recipe Direction

  1. Whisk mayonnaise
  2. Then Sugar
  3. Add Lemon Juice
  4. Mix with Vinegar
  5. Add Pepper
  6. Add Salt
  7. Mix all in a bowl until smooth and creamy
